Here is a concise summary of the meeting notes:

**Agenda Approval**: The Clearwater City Council approved the agenda for the October 10, 2022, meeting.

**Consent Items**:

1. Resolution 22-67: A $1,650 donation from the Alliance Club to fund gutters at the Wine's building.
